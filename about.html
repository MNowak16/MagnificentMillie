About Me

Hello, I'm Millie! I am approximately 2 years old and was formerly a feisty street cat. I was taken in to Lakeland Animal Shelter with two baby kittens. My little babies found their forever homes, and I was quickly adopted soon after. Now I am happy being a snuggly house kitty. I love playing with my toys, napping, and checking out birds outside.
